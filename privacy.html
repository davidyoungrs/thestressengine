Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Stress Engine ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding your use of our application.

2. Data Collection & Processing

The Stress Engine operates entirely as a client-side application. This means:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, engineering inputs, or calculation results to any servers.
  • Local Execution: All mathematical calculations are performed locally within your web browser.
  • No Accounts: We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any identifying information to use the application.

3. Third-Party Services

We use GitHub to host the source code and Vercel/Netlify for static site hosting. These infrastructure providers may collect standard anonymous access logs (such as IP addresses and user agents) for security and operational purposes, as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

4. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.

Cookies Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Stress Engine does not use any first-party cookies, tracking cookies, or analytics cookies.

  • No Tracking: We do not track your activity across other websites.
  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or similar third-party tracking software.
  • No Ads: We do not serve targeted advertisements.

3. Local Storage

To improve your experience, the application may eventually use your browser's localStorage API strictly to save your UI preferences (like unit system choices or dark mode settings). This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Cookies Policy, please reach out via our GitHub repository.

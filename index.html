Formula Reference Engine

Primary governing equations implemented in the Roark's Stress & Strain Calculator.

1. Flat Plates (Circular & Rectangular)

Bending stresses and deflections for flat plates under uniform transverse loads.

$$ \sigma_{max} = \frac{\beta q b^2}{t^2} $$ $$ y_{max} = \frac{\alpha q b^4}{E t^3} $$

Where $q$ is uniform pressure, $t$ is thickness, and $\alpha, \beta$ are Roark's tabulated coefficients based on boundary conditions.

2. Straight Beams

Governing differential equation of the deflection curve and flexure formulas.

$$ E I \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = M(x) $$ $$ \sigma_{bend} = \frac{M c}{I} $$ $$ \tau_{shear} = \frac{V Q}{I b} $$

3. Torsion

Angular twist and maximum shear stress for cross-sections under twisting moments.

$$ \theta = \frac{T L}{J G} $$ $$ \tau_{max} = \frac{T r}{J} \quad \text{(Solid Circular)} $$

4. Columns (Buckling)

Euler's critical load for long columns and the Secant formula for eccentric loading.

$$ P_{cr} = \frac{\pi^2 E I}{(K L)^2} $$ $$ \sigma_{max} = \frac{P}{A} \left[ 1 + \frac{e c}{r^2} \sec\left( \frac{K L}{2r} \sqrt{\frac{P}{E A}} \right) \right] $$

5. Contact Stresses (Hertzian)

Maximum contact pressure for two elastic bodies pressed together.

$$ p_{max} = \frac{3 F}{2 \pi a^2} \quad \text{(Point Contact)} $$ $$ p_{max} = \frac{2 F}{\pi b L} \quad \text{(Line Contact)} $$

Where $a$ and $b$ are the semi-axes of the contact ellipse or half-width of the contact area.

6. Dynamic Loads

Impact amplification factor and fundamental natural frequency.

$$ n = 1 + \sqrt{1 + \frac{2h}{y_{st}}} $$ $$ f_n = \frac{1}{2\pi} \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}} $$

7. Shells of Revolution

Membrane stresses for thin-walled spherical and cylindrical vessels.

$$ \sigma_{spherical} = \frac{p r}{2 t} $$ $$ \sigma_{hoop} = \frac{p r}{t} \quad \text{(Cylinder)} $$

8. Curved Beams

Winkler-Bach theory for non-linear stress distribution.

$$ e = \bar{r} - R $$ $$ \sigma = \frac{P}{A} + \frac{M}{A e} \left( \frac{y}{R + y} \right) $$
